Minecraft super flat worlds


1.15.1

how to use the superflat worlds:
1. go to singleplayer and click create new world.
2. go to more world options and click the world type until it says superflat.
3. under the world type it says customize, click customize.
4. click presets and copy a text below on this website and paste it on top in minecraft, and you should be good to go!
